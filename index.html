Welcome to Pat's!

We're so glad you're here! Our goal is to provide you with the greatest fish based cookies anywhere in the world. We started three years ago with just a mound of salmon, some batter and a dream. A dream that one day little people just like yourself would swim upstream to indulge in our fishy goodness!
Seriously, these cookies are good.
So come on in, take a look around. Order some swag and get to know us. We ahve several locations to suit your fish cookie needs.

The Randall - Named after our uncle Randall who back in the fall of 1978 rose to fame as the Upper South Northwest Regional Champion of Salmon punching.
